GTAVITIPS on November 19: The Launch Day Plan

The database goes live on launch day. Here is exactly what happens on November 19 and how the site grows from there.

GTAVITIPS April 2026
The Plan

What Happens on November 19, 2026

November 19, 2026 is the day Leonida goes live. It is also the day GTAVITIPS shifts from pre-launch intelligence to live coverage. This article explains exactly what the site will do on launch day and how the database will grow from that point forward.

The Database

From Pre-Launch to Live

The pre-launch database covers every confirmed entity from official footage. Launch day starts the process of filling in everything the trailers did not show. Every new location discovered, every vehicle confirmed, every character encountered, every activity unlocked will be added to the database as quickly as it can be verified.

The verification standard does not change on launch day. Confirmed still requires confirmation. Analysis is still labeled as analysis. The pace of additions will be higher than the pre-launch pace, but the standard for labeling stays constant.

Day One Priority List
The full Leonida map. New character roster. Complete vehicle list. All accessible locations. Mission structure overview without story spoilers. The wanted system mechanics. Activity unlock conditions.
The Map

Interactive Map Goes Live at Launch

The interactive Leonida map is targeting a launch-day release. The pre-launch version covers confirmed locations from official footage. The launch-day update adds exact in-game coordinates, collectible locations, activity spawn points, and everything else that requires being inside the game to document.

The map will be updated continuously in the weeks after launch as the community explores and the database expands.

Spoiler Policy

Story Spoilers Are Handled Separately

The GTAVITIPS database is built around the world, not the story. Character pages cover confirmed backgrounds and connections, not story outcomes. Location pages cover the geography and operational intelligence, not mission events. This policy holds on launch day. Players who want to experience the story without spoilers can use the full database safely.

Story-specific content will be clearly labeled and separated from the main database. The intelligence platform and the story spoiler content will never occupy the same space without explicit warning.
