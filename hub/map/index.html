Common Questions

Common Questions

When does the map go live?
November 19, 2026. The moment Leonida opens, so does the map. No preview, no soft launch — live the same day the servers come up.
What does the map cover?
Every location in Leonida, placed and linked. Faction territories, activity zones, and restricted areas. Built out continuously from launch day forward.
How many locations are on the map?
23 locations in the database now — from Vice City down to the Leonida Keys, inland to Mount Kalaga and Grassrivers. That number grows on and after November 19.
Can I browse locations before the map launches?
Yes. Every location has its own page in the database with all available intel. The interactive version drops November 19.
Is the map free?
Yes. Everything on GTAVITIPS is free.
