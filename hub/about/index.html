About GTAVITIPS

Last updated: May 19, 2026

What GTAVITIPS Is

GTAVITIPS is a GTA 6 intelligence database. Every confirmed entity from official Rockstar footage has a dedicated profile. Characters, vehicles, weapons, locations, animals, brands, factions, and activities. All sourced. All labeled by confidence level. Nothing speculated without a badge.

The database launched in May 2026. 322 profiles across eight categories. Everything is sourced from official Rockstar footage or approved industry sources. Speculation is labeled as such and kept well away from confirmed material.

How We Source Intel

Every claim on this site traces back to official material: Rockstar trailers, Rockstar Newswire, or a small set of proven industry insiders. All images and screenshots used on GTAVITIPS are sourced exclusively from official Rockstar releases.

Where the source is a single voice rather than confirmed across multiple outlets, that is noted. Where something is interpretation rather than fact, it is labeled as analysis. Nothing gets published as confirmed when it is not.

The Mission

GTA 6 releases November 19, 2026 on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S. GTAVITIPS was built to be the definitive source before launch and the deepest reference after it. The database does not pause when the game goes live. Every new mechanic, location, and character that the community discovers gets filed.

The goal is simple: if it happened in Leonida, it is in here.

Get in Touch

Tips, corrections, and press inquiries: info@gtavitips.com

If you have information from official sources that is not yet in the database, send it over. We verify before we publish. We label everything. We do not post what we cannot source.
