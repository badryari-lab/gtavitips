The World

The World

The state of Leonida. A fictional analog of modern Florida. Vice City is the main urban hub, based on Miami. The state covers a wide geographic range including the Leonida Keys, the Grassrivers wetlands, Port Gellhorn, and Mount Kalaga in the interior.

Source: Rockstar Trailer 1 and Trailer 2

No official map size has been released. Community estimates based on trailer footage place Leonida at approximately 120 to 140 square kilometres of playable space. GTA 5's map is estimated at around 81 square kilometres. If the estimates are accurate, Leonida is significantly larger. Full breakdown: map size rumours.

Source: Community analysis. unconfirmed

Read More →

Based on the confirmed geographic range of locations. Vice City, the Keys, Port Gellhorn, Grassrivers, Mount Kalaga, the rural interior. the map is almost certainly larger than GTA 5. The specific size has not been confirmed.

Source: Analysis from confirmed location geography

A second state called Gloriana, based on Georgia, has been mentioned in some reporting and community analysis. This has not been confirmed by Rockstar. The confirmed locations all sit within the Leonida state geography.

Source: GTAbase reporting. unconfirmed

Source: Rockstar Trailer 1 and Trailer 2

Story and Characters

Story and Characters

Lucia Caminos and Jason Duval are the two confirmed protagonists. Lucia is from Liberty City, released from a Leonida correctional facility at the story's start. Jason is a Leonida native with a military background and drug running history. Full profiles and all confirmed supporting characters are in the character database.

Source: Rockstar Trailer 1 and Trailer 2

The Trailer 2 description references a couple working together against the odds, which has led to widespread Bonnie and Clyde comparisons. Rockstar has not officially described the story as inspired by Bonnie and Clyde. The structural similarities. a criminal couple, a relationship-first framing, a crime-road premise. are clear. Whether it is a direct inspiration or a parallel structure is not confirmed.

Source: Analysis. Rockstar Trailer 2 description

Multiple sources including GamesRadar have described Jason and Lucia as a couple. Rockstar's own Trailer 2 description references their partnership in terms consistent with a romantic relationship. Rockstar has not used the word "romantic" in official communications. The framing in official material strongly implies it.

Source: Rockstar Trailer 2 description; industry reporting

The trailers consistently show both protagonists operating together in the same sequences rather than separately. This differs from GTA 5's character-switching model. The exact mechanic. whether you switch between them, control both simultaneously, or experience perspective shifts. has not been officially explained. Source: Analysis. no official mechanic explanation
