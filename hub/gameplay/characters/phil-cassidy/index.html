Profile Overview

Dossier

Phil Cassidy is back. The name has carried weight in this city since the first time this city existed on a screen. This is a different version of that character for a different era. This version of Vice City runs its own continuity. This Cassidy has both arms. The eccentricity and the hardware are both present.

He runs Phil's Ammu-Nation. A branded gun shop in Vice County that operates at the intersection of legal firearms retail and the kind of inventory that does not appear in any catalogue. His military background is the source of his product knowledge and his access. His personality is the reason people remember doing business with him.

For anyone in Leonida who needs firepower and prefers not to explain why to a chain retailer, Cassidy is the contact. He does not ask what the weapons are for. He has opinions about everything else.
